BLDC- Reinventing Ceiling Fan Motors

BLDC, a revolutionary technology in fans that combines efficiency, performance, and durability with design. Applying this cutting-edge technology, Colorbot BLDC fans bring to you better comfort at lesser energy consumption.

The motor uses Carbon brushless operation, resulting in a heatless, noiseless and carbon- less sustainable performance. Because of its energy efficiency, a Colorbot BLDC fan runs for a longer period of time on inverters.
